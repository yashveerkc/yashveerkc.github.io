Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

We respect your privacy. This bot collects no personal data and operates with minimal footprint.

No data is used for advertising, analytics, tracking, or sold to third parties. The bot exists solely for key validation.

Bot responses are marked as ephemeral (visible only to you) and disappear from chat history automatically.

  • No external tracking, cookies, or analytics tools are used.

5. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use after changes means acceptance.
